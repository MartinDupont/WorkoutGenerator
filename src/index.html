Random Workout Generator: Test Pipeline

This website will make random barbell workouts for you from templates. Just put in some search criteria and press go!

This site is mostly aimed at experienced weightlifters and bodybuilders. You should know your 1 rep maximum for the squat, deadlift, bench press and overhead press, and be able to at least estimate your 1RM for some other lifts.

This page is still in very early development. The selection criteria are limited, and the workout database is not very large. I hope to be adding more functionality soon.
